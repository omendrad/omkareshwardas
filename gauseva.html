Gau Seva

Selfless service to cows is a gateway to divine blessings and spiritual growth.

Why Gau Seva Matters

Gau Seva, or service to the sacred cow, holds a divine place in Sanatan Dharma. Cows are seen as mothers and nurturers — protectors of dharma and symbols of abundance. By serving and caring for cows, we invite peace, purity, and blessings into our lives.

“Where cows are worshipped, prosperity and purity naturally dwell.”

How You Can Serve

Join hands in this sacred mission:

  • Donate fodder or essentials
  • Volunteer for cleaning and care
  • Sponsor treatment for sick cows
  • Offer prayers and rituals on festive days
