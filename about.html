Omkareshwar Das Ji

@omkareshwardas

H.G. Omkareshwar Das Prabhuji has been devoted to the International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON) for more than 22 years, wholeheartedly dedicating his life to the propagation of Sri Krishna Bhakti.

In 2015, Prabhuji received his Guru-Diksha from His Holiness Radha Govind Goswami Maharaj, a divine milestone that infused his spiritual journey with deeper wisdom, devotion, and steadfast discipline.

In 2019, he founded the Sri Hare Krishna Jagannath Temple in Electronic City, Bengaluru—now a vibrant spiritual haven that inspires and serves thousands of devotees. As the Temple President, Prabhuji not only guides seekers on the path of devotion but also embodies it through his humility, service, and exemplary conduct.

His revered work, "Diksha Sutra," is a profound and touching guide for spiritual aspirants. It beautifully explains that diksha is not merely a ritual, but a sacred bond of love, faith, and surrender between the Guru and the disciple.

Prabhuji’s words carry simplicity, his wisdom carries depth, and his heart overflows with unwavering love for Sri Krishna. His life stands as a shining testimony to the truth that when one fully surrenders to the Supreme Lord, that very life becomes a lamp of inspiration for countless others.
